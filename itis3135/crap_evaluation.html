Website Evaluations


Good Website: Pure Hockey

Pure Hockey is a website where you can go buy hockey related stuff like gear or anything you would really need to play hockey.

  • Contrast: The contrast on the webstie is very clean. It is simple with a white background and black text. They also have different advertisments for the stuff they sell.
  • Repetition: There is a couple things on the website that repeats. The ads repeat and everythig is basically formatted the same.
  • Alignment: Depending on where it is on the website it is either centered or aligned to the left.
  • Proximity: The proximity of this website is also really good. Everything is even spaced out and doesnt seem to be on top of each other.

Bad Website: IMDb

IMDb is a website that helps you find information on movies that have been released.

  • Contrast: The colors of the website are not that bad. It is a dark background that has white text with a couple gold/yellow headings.
  • Repetition: The only repetition is that the headings are a golden yellow and all the other text is white.
  • Alignment: The alignment is all over the place. Some thing are aligned to the left, center and right.
  • Proximity: The proximity is really bad on this website. Everything is cramp and very close together.
